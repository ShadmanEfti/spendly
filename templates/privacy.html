Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect information you provide directly to us. This includes your email address and any financial data you choose to enter, such as expense amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates. We do not collect sensitive financial information like credit card numbers, bank account credentials, or social security numbers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services. Specifically, your data is used to: track and categorize your expenses, generate spending summaries and insights, send you service-related communications, and ensure the security of your account. We do not use your personal financial data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers with industry-standard encryption and security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, but only to the extent necessary to perform those functions. These third parties are obligated to keep your information confidential and secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can do this through your account settings or by contacting us. You may also export all your data in a machine-readable format. If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use minimal cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We may use anonymized analytics data to understand how users interact with our service, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@spendly.com.
